About FluffyPaws

Over here, we have beautiful and healthy puppies at reasonable prices. All puppies will go through proper veterinarian health checks, microchips and 2 vaccinations before going home. Puppies will be dewormed to ensure optimal nutrition absorption and growth. Getting a puppy is a lifetime of joy and love, not a spur of the moment. Our company will continue to be in touch with all owners in order to educate and advise them in times of need and queries.
